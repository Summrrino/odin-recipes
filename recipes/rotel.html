Rotel Dip

Description

Rotel Dip is a quick and easy party favorite that’s sure to please. This creamy, cheesy dip is made with just a few simple ingredients: perfectly spiced hamburger, Velveeta cheese, and full-flavored Rotel tomatoes. Rotel tomatoes are canned diced tomatoes with green chilies, adding a touch of spice to the dip. The dip is prepared by browning the ground beef, then adding in the Rotel tomatoes and cheese, and stirring until melted. It’s served warm, often with tortilla chips, crackers, or veggies for dipping. This dip is perfect for game day, parties, or any gathering where you want to serve a crowd-pleasing appetizer. Enjoy a bowl of Rotel Dip for a taste of a classic party favorite right at home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound premium bulk sausage
  • 1 pound processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), cubed
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chilies (such as RO-TEL® Original), undrained

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, breaking up sausage with a spatula as it cooks, about 10 minutes. Drain sausage and return to the skillet.
  2. Add Velveeta® and RO-TEL®. Reduce heat to medium and cook until fully melted and combined, about 15 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes. For a thinner dip, add a splash of half and half in the last 5 minutes of cook time. Serve immediately.
